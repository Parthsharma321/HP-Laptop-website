Common Questions

Is the RAM upgradeable on the HP 245 G10?
Yes, the HP 245 G10 features two SODIMM slots, allowing you to upgrade the memory up to 32 GB DDR4-3200 MHz depending on your configuration.
Does it support multiple external monitors?
Yes, thanks to the HDMI 1.4b port and the USB Type-C® port (which supports DisplayPort™), you can connect multiple external displays to expand your workspace.
Does it come with pre-installed MS Office?
Yes, select configurations come with Microsoft Office Home & Student pre-installed. Please check the product configuration summary for local details.
What is the warranty coverage in India?
The laptop comes with a 1-year standard onsite warranty. HP has a wide service network across India, and you can also purchase extended warranty packs (HP Care Pack) for up to 3 years.
Can I play casual games on this laptop?
Yes, the integrated AMD Radeon™ Graphics can handle casual games like Minecraft, League of Legends, and CS:GO at low-to-medium settings smoothly, though it is not a dedicated gaming laptop.
What security features are included?
The laptop comes equipped with HP Wolf Security for Business, which includes hardware-enforced security features to protect against BIOS-level attacks and malware.
How fast does the battery charge?
With HP Fast Charge technology, you can go from 0 to 50% charge in approximately 45 minutes, ensuring you stay powered up even on the go.
